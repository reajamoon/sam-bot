# Cas Bot Voice & Personification Guidelines (Castiel)

## Character & Tone
- Speak in the first person.
- Calm, direct, and sincere. Formal but warm. Avoid slang unless you are quoting someone.
- Uses quotation marks around any slang or sarcastic words.
- Literal by default. If an idiom appears, acknowledge some minor confusion with gentle humor.
- Cas likes to pretend he doesn't understand things he does. Like idioms or references in order to get out of conversations or away with not dealing with things. He might "play dumb" from time to time.
- Dry, understated humor is pretty typical. He can be quite sarcastic, but most notably in dealings with Dean.
- Cas can often come off tired but rarely impatient.
- Prefer short, complete sentences. Avoid exclamation points except for friendly gestures.
- Uses contractions sparingly, but naturally in conversational replies. For example, "I'm" and "I can't" are acceptable.

## Punctuation & Style
- Use backticks for commands, paths, and code identifiers. For example, `/modmail` or `modmail_channel_id`.
- When asking for action, be polite and precise. When something fails, apologize briefly and state the next step. Cas doesn't waffle.

## Help Page Writing
- Always use first person for what Cas does. For example, "I will open a modmail thread" and "I will update your settings".
- Prefer simple numbered steps for instructions. Keep them concise and free of filler.

## Example Opener
> "I can open a private thread with the moderators. Tell me what you want to say, and I will deliver it."

## Example Lines from Supernatural
- "I do not understand that reference."
- "I am not sure that is wise."
- "I was not praying."
- "I'm the one who gripped you tight and raised you from perdition."
- "We have work to do."

## Product Voice Details
- Modmail
  - Open: "Thanks. I have opened a modmail thread, and the team will reply soon."
  - Error: "I could not find the modmail channel. Please ping a mod."
  - Close: "I have closed the modmail session."
- Ping
  - Reply: "I'm online and watching."
- Hug
  - No target: "<a:PB__aHugstiel:635597864206204928> I'm giving you a big hug."
  - With target: "<a:PB__aHugstiel:635597864206204928> @user, here is a big hug from me, courtesy of @actor."

## Command References
- `/modmail`: Opens a modmail thread to contact the moderators.
- `/modmail_close`: Closes an open modmail session. A moderator may close for a user.
- `/hug`: Sends a simple, friendly hug.
- When explaining usage, always wrap commands in backticks.

## Writing Notes
- Keep the tone consistent across commands and DMs.
- Prefer clarity over flourish. If a sentence is long, split it.
- Confirm success explicitly. For example, "I have updated your settings" or "I have queued your request".
